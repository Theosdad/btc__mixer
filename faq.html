Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to use the Mixer service?

    Enter Destination BTC Addresses: Provide one or more destination BTC addresses for the transaction. These addresses represent where you want your BTC to be sent after the mixing process.
    Choose Fees: Select the fees that you would like Mixer to deduct for the service. The available fee options and their functions are explained in detail on our «About» page. Make sure to review this information for a better understanding.

  • How does Mixer work?

    Mixer is a trusted BTC transaction service dedicated to enhancing the security and privacy of your transactions. Our primary goal is to make your transactions highly intricate to track, ultimately strengthening your online identity and security.

  • What is Mixer’s guarantee?

    On the transaction page, you have the option to download your Letter of Guarantee. This document serves as your unique proof of transaction, as it contains a signed record of your transaction details. Should you encounter any issues or have inquiries regarding the status of your transactions, feel free to reach out to our support team at example@example.com

  • How does the Mixer protocol work?

    Mixer employs advanced and sophisticated protocols to provide highly secure transactions. Our primary objective is to ensure the confidentiality and anonymity of your online transactions. To achieve this, we leverage the power of the widely recognized CoinJoin protocol. However, it’s important to note that we have developed our own unique strategies and algorithms, tailored specifically to meet the high standards of privacy and security that our users expect. By utilizing these proprietary protocols, we offer a level of protection and anonymity that goes beyond traditional CoinJoin implementations. Your virtual identity remains safeguarded, and your transactions remain hidden from prying eyes on the internet.

  • Why should I protect and anonymize my transactions?

    Ensuring your online safety and protecting your financial assets is of utmost importance. By prioritizing the security and confidentiality of your transactions, you shield yourself from potential threats posed by malicious hackers and other cybercriminals. Maintaining the anonymity of your transactions is equally crucial, as it allows you to keep the origin and details of your financial activities private. In an unpredictable and potentially hazardous digital landscape, it is essential to exercise caution and prudence when sending and receiving money. By adopting these practices, you establish a safeguard against unauthorized access and preserve your financial well-being in the ever-evolving world of technology.

  • What are the limits for sending transactions?

    To initiate a transaction, a minimum amount of 0.002 BTC is required, while the maximum allowable limit is set at 100.00 BTC. It’s important to note that if you send an amount below the minimum requirement, the funds will not reach their intended destination due to transaction fees and may be treated as a donation instead.

  • How does the advanced mode operate?

    Our advanced mode operates using a unique approach to cryptocurrency mixing known as swapping. This method involves exchanging funds from BTC to XMR (Monero) and then back to BTC. By utilizing automatically generated wallets that are independent of any exchange, we ensure a high level of transaction privacy and make it significantly challenging to trace the movement of funds.
