Our Services Fees

Choosing higher fees provides your transaction with a higher priority level, ensuring faster processing. Each fee value corresponds to a different protocol that facilitates quicker transactions. On average, transactions with higher fees experience a delay of approximately one hour, while those with lower fees may encounter a delay of around 3 hours. However, the exact delay can vary due to blockchain dynamics.

To support the continuous improvement of our protocols and the addition of new features, we charge a fixed fee of 0.0003 BTC. Please note that the minimum amount to send is 0.002 BTC, and the maximum is 100.00 BTC. Transactions below the minimum threshold will not be processed and will be treated as a donation.

No Logs Policy

At Mixer, we prioritize the confidentiality of your transactions, and as part of our commitment to privacy, we do not retain any records of your requests within our service.

Once your session expires, which typically lasts for 24 hours, we ensure that no traces of your visit are stored or kept. Our aim is to provide a secure and anonymous environment for your transactions.

However, please note that we do retain transaction information until it is completed and confirmed. This allows us to assist you in case of any troubleshooting or issues that may arise. Rest assured that this information is handled with the utmost care and is only used for necessary support purposes.

  • CoinJoin

    It is important to be aware that BTC transactions on the blockchain are inherently transparent, which can compromise the confidentiality of your transactions.

    To address this issue, Mixer employs an advanced algorithm known as CoinJoin, which operates based on the priority order of your transaction fees. When users initiate transactions, Mixer ensures their privacy by combining and mixing transactions from multiple users simultaneously. To further enhance privacy, Mixer utilizes different wallets as gateways, ensuring that previously mixed funds cannot be traced back to individual users. While users may have the impression that their transactions are easily traceable, the origin of the obtained funds remains hidden and confidential.

    By implementing this protocol, Mixer ensures that clients no longer need to worry about their new transactions getting mixed up with previous ones. This approach guarantees the confidentiality of your blockchain transactions, providing you with the peace of mind and privacy you seek.

  • Advanced Mode

    Our advanced mode utilizes a unique mixing method in the realm of cryptocurrencies known as «exchange.» This method involves exchanging funds from BTC to XMR (Monero) and then back to BTC. To ensure utmost privacy and security, we employ automatically generated wallets that are not associated with any exchange, making it extremely challenging to trace the transactions.

    By incorporating the privacy-centric cryptocurrency Monero, our process becomes impervious to chain analysis, rendering it impossible to establish links with Monero addresses. Additionally, the funds are fragmented into random pieces, making it virtually impossible to correlate the exchanged amount. The end result is a transaction that is practically untraceable, passing through several wallets before reaching the intended destination address.

    At Mixer, we take great pride in our advanced mode and highly recommend its use. We firmly believe that it offers a higher level of privacy compared to the CoinJoin protocol and encourage users to leverage its capabilities. It is important to note that the advanced mode requires a fixed fee and a single destination address due to its intricate design. Rest assured, we are continuously working towards enhancing and refining this mode for even greater user experience in the future.
